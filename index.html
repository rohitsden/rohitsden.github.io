About

Operator first, writer second.

My perspective comes from the messy middle: complex customers, technical products, support escalations, engineering tradeoffs, and the daily work of making teams better.

I lead Customer Success and Support at Cerebras. My work centers on translating customer complexity into durable operating models: cleaner escalation paths, better knowledge flows, stronger customer outcomes, and teams that can scale without losing judgment.

I’m especially interested in how AI changes the internal machinery of post-sales teams — what gets automated, what gets amplified, and what still requires human taste, trust, and technical depth.

Personal site. Views are my own. Built as a home for essays, notes, and conversations about AI and customer-facing technical teams.
